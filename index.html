How ProfAI is built

How ProfAI is built.

Each ProfAI report is prepared using several structured birth-data modalities, then translated into clear practical language.

AI supports drafting, comparison and synthesis. The report structure, methodology and review process are professionally built, with the aim of giving the reader useful themes to reflect on and test in real life.

Who this is for

ProfAI may be useful if you are:

a parent helping a child or teenager understand strengths and subject fit;
a student choosing study direction;
a young adult deciding between pathways;
an adult exploring a career pivot or clearer professional direction;
someone who wants a structured starting point before making a bigger decision.

For urgent medical, psychological, legal, financial, therapeutic, admissions, employment or crisis decisions, use qualified professional support and the relevant official or specialist process.

How to use the report

How to use the report.

ProfAI helps organise patterns, strengths, learning conditions and direction hypotheses into a clear written report.

The best use of the report is as a starting point for reflection, research, conversation and practical testing. Final education, study or career decisions remain with the client and family.

For medical, psychological, legal, financial, therapeutic, investment, admissions or employment decisions, use the relevant qualified professional, official or specialist advice alongside any ProfAI reflection.
